Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Small Chat is an on-device AI chat application. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Small Chat does not require an account, does not track usage, and does not collect any information about you or your device.

How the App Works

All AI inference runs entirely on your device. Your conversations, messages, and prompts are never transmitted to any server. Everything stays local.

Internet Usage

The app connects to the internet only to browse and download AI model files from Hugging Face (huggingface.co). No personal data is attached to these requests. Once a model is downloaded, it runs fully offline.

Local Storage

Downloaded model files and your chat history are stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data.

Third Parties

Small Chat does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third party.

Children

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
